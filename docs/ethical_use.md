# Ethical Use Policy

## AAPP-MART

### 1. Purpose and Intended Use

AAPP-MART is designed exclusively for **authorized security assessments and defensive cybersecurity operations**. The platform leverages artificial intelligence to support attack path prediction, modeling, and simulation in order to help organizations better understand, evaluate, and reduce their attack surfaces.

The tool is intended for use in:

- Controlled and authorized environments
- Defensive security research
- Threat modeling initiatives
- Red team / blue team exercises
- Security architecture validation

Under no circumstances is AAPP-MART intended for facilitating unauthorized access, malicious intrusion, or harmful cyber activities.

Users are solely responsible for ensuring full compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, contractual obligations, and internal governance policies when using this tool.

---

### 2. Intended Audience

AAPP-MART is developed for use by qualified cybersecurity professionals, including but not limited to:

- Security Operations Center (SOC) teams
- Red Team and Blue Team professionals
- Penetration testers
- Security architects
- Threat modeling specialists
- Cybersecurity researchers
- Academic researchers and students in cybersecurity disciplines

Use by individuals outside these contexts must still adhere strictly to authorized and lawful parameters.

---

### 3. Explicit Non-Objectives

AAPP-MART is **not** designed to function as:

- An exploit development framework
- A malware distribution platform
- A zero-day weaponization system
- A destructive or uncontrolled attack tool

Any attempt to repurpose the platform for offensive, unlawful, or malicious objectives directly contradicts its intended design and ethical framework.

---

### 4. Legal and Ethical Responsibility

Operation of AAPP-MART against unauthorized targets or within uncontrolled environments may constitute illegal activity. Such use is expressly condemned.

Users must ensure that:

- Explicit authorization has been granted prior to testing
- All assessments are conducted within legal and contractual boundaries
- Activities align with recognized ethical cybersecurity standards

The developers and maintainers of AAPP-MART disclaim liability for any misuse, unlawful deployment, or damages arising from improper application of the tool.
