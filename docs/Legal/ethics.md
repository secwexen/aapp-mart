# Ethics & Responsible Use Guidelines

## Purpose
 
This document outlines ethical guidelines for using the repository and its content.

## Prohibited Actions

Users **must not use** AAPP-MART for:

- Unauthorized attacks against live systems or networks.
- Causing damage, disruption, or data loss.
- Exploiting vulnerabilities in systems without explicit permission.
- Any illegal or malicious activity.

All actions should be performed **only in controlled, simulated, or lab environments**.

## Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability or bug:

- Report it **responsibly to the project maintainers** or authorized channels.
- Do **not publicly disclose** the vulnerability before it is addressed.
- Avoid using any discovered vulnerabilities for malicious purposes.

Responsible disclosure ensures a safer and ethical research environment.

## Research & Educational Use

- Use AAPP-MART for **learning, testing, and research purposes only**.
- Experiments must be conducted in **isolated environments**.
- Avoid exposing real users, systems, or sensitive data to risk.
- Document and share findings responsibly to contribute to knowledge.

## Compliance & Legal Considerations

- Users are responsible for **complying with local laws and regulations**.
- Using AAPP-MART **does not absolve legal responsibility**.
- Organizations must follow internal policies when running tests or simulations.

## Acknowledgement

By using this repository, you acknowledge and agree that:

- You understand the ethical guidelines.
- You will not use AAPP-MART for unauthorized, illegal, or harmful activities.
- You accept responsibility for any actions performed with the repository.
